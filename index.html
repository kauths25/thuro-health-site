What we do

Thuro Health LLC is a health-technology company. We build software that gathers your health information — from wearables, apps, and lab results — into one private place, helps you make sense of it over time, and flags when something may be worth a doctor's attention.

All in one place

Your data from the devices and apps you already use, unified into a single view.

Made understandable

Plain-language context that turns raw numbers into something you can actually act on.

A gentle heads-up

When a reading looks worth checking, Thuro points you toward a professional — it never replaces one.

Thuro is a wellness and information tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and is not a substitute for professional medical care.
